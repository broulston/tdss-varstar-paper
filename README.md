The Time-Domain Spectroscopic Survey (TDSS) is a Sloan Digital Sky Survey-IV (SDSS-IV) subprogramm aimed at performing initial characterization and identification of photometrically-variable point sources. The TDSS sample was selected and targeted based on variability in the PS1 3Ï€ and SDSS surveys.

This paper describes the stellar population of the TDSS sample.
